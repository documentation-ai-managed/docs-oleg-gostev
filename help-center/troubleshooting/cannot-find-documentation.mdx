description: How to quickly locate your docs.
---

## Symptoms

- Unable to find a project or topic
- Search returns no results

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Use the Search Bar">
    Make sure you enter specific keywords or project names in the search bar at the top.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse Categories">
    Explore categories from the homepage to see all available documentation sections.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Support">
    If you're still lost, email support@oleggostev.com for assistance.
  </Step>
</Steps>
