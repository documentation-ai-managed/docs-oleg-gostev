## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Safari)
- An email address for account verification
- Internet connection

<Callout kind="info">

Oleg Gostev is a web-based platform. No downloads or installations required.

</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Follow these steps to sign up and log in.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="globe">
    Open your browser and navigate to `https://dashboard.example.com`.

    Click the **Sign Up** button in the top right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user-plus">
    Provide your email address, create a strong password, and complete the CAPTCHA.

    Click **Create Account**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email and Log In" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Oleg Gostev.

    Click the verification link, then log in with your credentials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">

Save your credentials securely. Use a password manager for best security.

</Callout>

## Set Up Your First Documentation Space

Once logged in, create a space to organize your docs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create New Space" icon="plus">
    From the dashboard, click **New Space**.

    Enter a name like "My Project Docs" and select a brand color (`#3B82F6` for Oleg Gostev blue).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Space Settings" icon="settings">
    Set visibility to **Private** or **Public**, add a description, and save.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add Your First Document

Spaces use MDX for rich documentation. Create your first page.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="MDX Editor" icon="edit-3">
    In your space, click **New Document**.

    Use the built-in editor to write:

````mdx
---
title: Welcome
description: My first document.
---

## Hello World

This is your first MDX page in Oleg Gostev!
````
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Upload Markdown" icon="upload">
    Prepare a file like `welcome.md`:

````markdown
# Welcome

This is plain Markdown converted to MDX.
````

    Drag and drop into the space or use **Import**.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

Preview and publish your document.
